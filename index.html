Objectives

This table contains objectives. Each objective has an importance, that is, how much does this objective mean to you. I try to use a 1-5 scale for importance.

Results

The program does some math, and tells you how your options measure up to your criteria. Try changing the importance of some of your objectives and see how these change! For example, if you are looking at the default values, make the importance of cost 5. U of H now is ahead!

Options

Each of your options goes here. For each option, rank how well that options meets each objective. For example, if an objective in a decision of mine were "fun" and one of my options was "decision making" I would place a 5 in the box corresponding to that objective, in that option's row. Either a 1-5 or 1-7 scale works well here.
